This feature is the Switch Mode system (Accounts). It exists on both the Home Page and the Profile Page, with different functionality.

Home Page (Switching Only)
	•	Located directly under the top bar
	•	Displays a horizontal row of account pills
	•	The leftmost pill is the active account (highlighted)
	•	Additional accounts appear to the right

Visual:
	•	Pills use a banner-style image representing the work/brand, not avatars

Interaction:
	•	Tapping a pill:
	•	Switches the active account
	•	Updates the entire app experience (Home, Profile, timeline, messages, etc.)

No ability to add accounts here.

⸻

Profile Page (Switching + Adding Accounts)

Layout
	•	Section labeled “Switch Mode”
	•	Directly underneath is a single long pill:
	•	Left: Active account name (updates when switched)
	•	Right: “Switch or Add Account” in blue text (text button, no background)

Overlay
	•	Opens as a full overlay panel with an X (top-left)
	•	Contains two separate rounded sections (bubbles):

Current Accounts
	•	Full-width stacked rows
	•	Each includes a banner-style image (not avatar)
	•	Tap → switches account and closes overlay

Add Account Types
	•	Full-width stacked rows
	•	Homeowner
	•	Collaborator
	•	Trade Pro
	•	Facilities Pro
	•	Trade Pro “Teammate”
	•	Facility Pro “Teammate”
	•	Tap → routes immediately into account creation flow

Scroll Behavior
	•	The dropdown/overlay itself does not scroll internally
	•	All options should be visible within the panel (expected max ~10 items)
	•	Any scrolling that occurs should be the main app screen behind/with the panel, not inside the bubbles

Routing
	•	Homeowner → Add Property
	•	Trade Pro → Add Business
	•	Facilities Pro → Add Business (first step)

⸻

Terminology Note (Needs Review)
	•	Homeowner may include: owner, caretaker, landlord, renter, manager
	•	Trade Pro: architect, designer, contractor, handyman, plumber, painter, etc.
	•	Facilities Pro: company owner, facility supervisor, landlord
	•	Trade Pro “Teammate”: employee of a company
	•	Facility Pro “Teammate”: maintenance, cleaning crews, landscaping, laundry, etc.
	•	Collaborator: neutral individual account for connecting and viewing projects
