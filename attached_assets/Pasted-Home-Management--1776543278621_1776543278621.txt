  3. Recurring task scheduling & accountability — Define cadenced objectives for domestic staff

  reminders                                               
  4. Structured collaboration windows — Support compressed 10-15 minute check-ins with trade    
  professionals via pre-built agendas and action plans so short meetings are productive         
  5. Work visibility & progress tracking — Make completed and in-progress work visible to the
  homeowner without requiring micromanagement                                                   
  6. Handoff continuity — When a provider is replaced, their work history, notes, and context
  transfer cleanly to the successor                                                             
  7. Multi-property support — Manage multiple homes from a single view
