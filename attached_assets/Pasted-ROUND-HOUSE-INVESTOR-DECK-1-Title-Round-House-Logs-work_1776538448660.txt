10 — Business Model

The platform starts free.

Users can:
• Create unlimited property profiles
• Log work
• Build a private record

Free is designed for capture and personal use.

Pro unlocks coordination.

⸻

11 — Pro Access — Per Property

Pro is applied at the property level.

Each property unlocks coordination, visibility, and tools.

⸻

Pro Home — $30/month per property
• Shared timeline with providers
• Coordination across service providers
• Full property record

⸻

Pro Commercial — $100/month per location
• Multi-user coordination
• Work orders
• Standardized records

⸻

Each property operates independently.
Pro is tied to the property, not the user.

⸻

12 — Payment Logic

Free → private capture across unlimited properties

Pro → shared coordination on a property

Upgrade happens when a property requires:
• sharing
• collaboration
• visibility across people

Sharing is the paid action.

24 — Why Now

We’ve reached a scale where work can no longer be managed informally.

More properties.
More moving parts.
More people involved.

But the systems haven’t caught up.

Work is still being tracked through texts, memory, and scattered tools.

At this level, things break:
• missed details
• lost history
• no accountability

Round House exists because this is no longer manageable without a system.
