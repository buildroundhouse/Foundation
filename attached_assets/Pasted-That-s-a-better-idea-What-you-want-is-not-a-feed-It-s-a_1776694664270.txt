Better timeline structure

Instead of stacked posts, use a vertical center line down the screen.

Layout
	•	a wire line in the middle
	•	date or time markers placed along the line
	•	bubbles/cards branching off left and right
	•	each bubble represents a moment, update, or work event

Each bubble can show:
	•	one preview photo
	•	short title
	•	short note
	•	timestamp
	•	project or client label

Then when tapped, it expands to show:
	•	more photos
	•	more notes
	•	attachments
	•	related work log details

⸻

Best behavior

Collapsed state

Each timeline event shows:
	•	thumbnail
	•	event title
	•	date/time
	•	one-line summary

Expanded state

When opened, it reveals:
	•	photo gallery
	•	full note
	•	linked receipt / invoice / task if relevant
	•	who posted it

⸻

Time grouping

Use day markers on the center line.

Example:
	•	Today
	•	Yesterday
	•	Monday, April 14
	•	Saturday, April 12

Under each date marker, events branch out as bubbles.

That gives it structure and avoids the cluttered social-feed look.

⸻

Why this is better

It makes the app feel like:
	•	a work record
	•	a job history
	•	a progress journal

Not like Instagram.

That fits your app much better.
